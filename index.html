Guru Travels is a trusted name in comfortable, reliable cab and tour services across Tamil Nadu. With a fleet of well-maintained vehicles — from sedans to Force Travellers — we serve individuals, families, and corporate groups with equal care and dedication.

Founded over a decade ago, we have built our reputation on punctuality, transparent pricing, and a deep knowledge of local routes and destinations across South India.

27

⛩ 27 Divya Desams in One Stretch

Our signature 27 Divya Desams pilgrimage circuit takes devotees through all 27 sacred Vishnu temples in Tamil Nadu in a single seamless journey — covering Kumbakonam, Mayiladuthurai, Sirkazhi, and surrounding temple towns without a break in comfort or devotion.

108

🪔 108 Divya Darisanam Circuit

We are proud specialists of the 108 Divya Desams — the complete set of Vishnu temples across India, including those in Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Andhra Pradesh, and beyond. Our experienced drivers have completed this sacred circuit multiple times and guide you at every step.
